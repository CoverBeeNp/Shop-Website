Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about our products, delivery, or return process? We’ve got answers below. Click on each to expand.

General Questions

What types of covers do you sell?
We offer iPhone covers for models starting from iPhone 11. Our collection includes Classic, Artistic, Luxury, and seasonal designs — crafted for both durability and fashion. We can also provide covers for models older than iPhone 11 upon request, but delivery may take longer.
How do I place an order?
Currently, we don’t have an online checkout. This website is meant to showcase our collection and provide a place for customers to view all available covers. If you're interested, simply DM us on Instagram with the case you're interested in.
Can I request a custom design or photo cover?
Unfortunately, we don’t currently offer custom designs. However, if there's enough interest, we may provide this service in the future at an additional cost.
Do you sell covers for Android phones?
Right now, we focus on iPhone covers. That said, we may be able to make custom Android covers on request — just message us to check availability.
Are the covers protective or just for looks?
Our covers are built for both style and protection. They’re made from durable materials that help absorb minor impacts and prevent scratches.
Do you sell cover bundles or offer discounts on multiple purchases?
Yes! We occasionally run promos or bundle deals. Follow us on Instagram to stay updated on offers.

Shipping

Do you provide free home delivery?
Yes, we do offer free shipping, but it only covers delivery to the nearest courier office at your location. Home delivery is not included in the free shipping offer. However, you can add home delivery for an additional fee of Rs. 50 when placing your order.
How long does shipping take?
Typically, shipping takes 2–4 business days depending on your location and the availability of stock.
Do you deliver outside of Chitwan?
Yes, we deliver all over Nepal using trusted courier partners.
Can I track my order?
Yes! We’ll keep you updated throughout the process. Once your package reaches your nearest courier office, you’ll get a call or text notifying you it's ready for pickup.

Returns

Do you accept returns?
Currently, we are unable to accept any returns. Please double-check your phone model and order details before placing an order.

You have different questions?

Our team will answer all your questions. We ensure a quick response.
